About Me

MBA Candidate | IIM Lucknow

I am an MBA candidate at the Indian Institute of Management, Lucknow with a strong foundation in finance, accounting, and business analytics. Prior to my MBA, I worked as a Research Analyst at Paperpedia Pvt. Ltd., where I developed accounting and finance learning modules for international universities. My interests include Corporate Finance, Equity Research, Financial Modelling, ESG, Infrastructure, and Data Analytics.
